I thrive when making a positive impact, working with dynamic, diverse teams to develop business and product strategies, then collaborating with them to build products that are useful and valuable to the business. The Resume and Projects sections below provide highlights of my career as well as an overview of my leadership style and the product management methodologies and techniques my teams and I have used to deliver exceptional products that drive business growth. This includes the following areas of my expertise:

  • Product strategy and roadmapping
    Creating product visions, roadmaps, and data-driven strategies that are aligned with business goals. Designing systems that enable teams to focus on the right work for customers and the business, and prioritizing features that drive customer satisfaction and revenue growth.
